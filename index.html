F20F FOUNDATION

Structural human transformation through ethical leadership, discipline, and the revolutionary Factor 20 methodology.

Institutional Identity

The F20F Foundation (Factor 20) is an international entity dedicated to the structural transformation of the human being. Our operational architecture bridges the institutional strength of the United States with the resilient spirit of Bolivia to restore dignity to the most vulnerable.

Mission

Our mission is to transform lives through the structural reengineering of human will using the proprietary FACTOR 20 methodology. We combine the energy of youth with the discipline of experienced leaders to restore dignity to animals, children, and the elderly while forming principled leaders free from addictions and committed to truth and responsibility.

The Factor 20 Method

The Factor 20 methodology proposes that real transformation occurs during the 20 silent days before visible change. This process moves individuals from vulnerability to conscious power, forging discipline, character, ethical leadership and long-term transformation.

Strategic Lines of Action

Animal Rescue

Medical care, sterilization and rescue programs for abandoned animals.

Positive Leadership

Formation of disciplined leaders based on the 0-Alcohol principle.

Women Leadership

Empowering women as agents of transformation and crisis leadership.

Media Truth

Education to identify misinformation and defend democratic integrity.

Food Sovereignty

Urban hydroponics education programs for youth communities.

Nature & Culture

Connecting youth with art, philosophy and nature to develop deep thinking.

The Flaca Effect

The emotional origin of the foundation lies in Bolivia with the rescue of Flaca, a dog found in a landfill near death. Her recovery proved that love, discipline and method can rebuild life. If Flaca could be rebuilt, human dignity and leadership can be rebuilt as well.
