Mission

The Transformational Mandate

The mission of the F20F Foundation is to transform lives through the structural reengineering of the human will using the proprietary Factor 20 methodology.

The foundation promotes ethical leadership, youth development, humanitarian action, and responsible social participation as the pillars of a disciplined and transformative society.

Factor 20 Method

Manifesto of Intrinsic Transformation

Factor 20 is a structured methodology that proposes a silent transformation period before visible behavioral change occurs. It focuses on strengthening the will, consolidating ethical discipline, and creating sustainable personal transformation.

Through a structured 20-day cycle, individuals move from vulnerability toward conscious self-leadership and responsible social action.

The 20-Day Architecture

Days 1-5 Foundation
Days 6-10 Ignition
Days 11-15 Social Structure
Days 16-20 Consolidation
Day 21 Permanent Transformation
