Institutional Identity

A Global Hub for Transformation

F20F Foundation is an international institution dedicated to the structural transformation of the human being. Its institutional architecture connects the operational solidity of the United States with the resilient social spirit of Bolivia.

The foundation is designed as a platform where discipline, experience, potential, and ethical action converge to restore dignity to vulnerable lives and generate principled leadership.

Mission

The Transformational Mandate

Our mission is to transform lives through the structural reengineering of the human will using the proprietary Factor 20 methodology.

We combine the energy of youth with the discipline of experienced leadership to restore dignity to animals, children, and the elderly, while forming leaders rooted in truth, responsibility, and ethical self-command.

The foundation advances addiction-free leadership, the empowerment of women as agents of change, and the defense of media truth as a vital democratic responsibility.

Origin

The Flaca Effect

Bolivia is the emotional heart of the foundation. The symbolic origin of F20F lies in the rescue of Flaca, a dog discovered in catastrophic condition and restored through love, care, and determined action.

That event became institutional proof that reconstruction is possible. If life could be rebuilt in Flaca, dignity and leadership can be rebuilt in the human spirit as well.

“Love changes everything. It transforms everything.”
